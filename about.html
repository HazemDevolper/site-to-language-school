Who Are We?

Skolera(formerly Bedopedia) is a Unified Learning Platform that utilizes the latest educational approaches and technology. It’s structured to provide a base on which you can drive a comprehensive academic and administrative operation for managing K-12 education institutions. Using various unconnected systems to carry out the tasks in educational institutions was confusing, time consuming, and often burdensome. Skolera tackles this by encompassing learning management, content management and a school management systems all in one place.
OUR MISSION

To instil the desire to teach and learn using a unified learning platform, with an addictive friendly interface, seamless navigation, and an indispensable bundle of features.

OUR VISION

To empower educators with smart and comprehensive educational solutions, inspiring everyone to surpass their limits. We aim to channel students’ passion for technology towards a better constructive education.

OUR GOAL

To unleash the power of digital computing and cloud-based learning to exceed educators’ expectations and fulfil, today, their dreams of tomorrow. To provide affordable and scalable technology that teachers, students, parents and administrators will love to use

A TEAM OF INNOVATORS

The Skolera team is dedicated to offering you cutting edge solutions and assuring that you are at the forefront of education. We strive to make a positive difference and great change that will benefit you.
